Spinny Connect Four

Welcome to Spinny Connect Four!

A 4x4 grid game where after each move:

  • The inner 2x2 grid rotates 90° clockwise
  • The outer ring pieces each shift one space counter-clockwise

The first player to connect 4 pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!
